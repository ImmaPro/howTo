Breakfast Dinner

American Breakfast

Ingredients

  1. 2 Eggs
  2. 1/2 Tbsp Butter
  3. 1 Slice Bread
  4. Bacon
  5. Sausage
  6. (Optional, Orange Juice)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°, while waiting for that, put parchment paper on a cookie sheet and put bacon ontop, when oven is preheated put the bacon in oven for 20 minutes
  2. Turn pan on to medium heat, when hot put sausage on, wait until the first side is brown and crispy and flip, repeat and take off heat
  3. When bacon has 3 minutes left, turn stove on to medium heat, put butter in pan and wait until melted, pour eggs in and cook until done
  4. When eggs are almost done, put bread in the toaster, butter the toast, eat (Optional, Orange Juice)

Spaghetti

Ingredients

  1. 1 Lb. Ground Beef
  2. Half Box of Noodles
  3. Half Can of Tomato Sauce

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil, pour noodles in, stirring often
  2. When noodles are slightly soft, cook ground beef until everything is brown
  3. When noodles are done, drain and put back in pot, pour tomato sauce in with ground beef and cook on low for 2 minutes
  4. Eat.
